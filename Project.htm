Recipe 1 - BBQ


Quick Instructions:


In a small bowl, whisk all the glaze ingredients together.
Toss the cubed chicken in the mixture and let it rest for 15 minutes.
Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto small bamboo skewers (pre-soaked in water).
Heat your grill or a non-stick pan over medium-high heat.
Cook the skewers for 3 to 4 minutes per side, brushing with any leftover marinade,
until the chicken is slightly charred and fully cooked through.


Recipe-2 - Butter Chicken


Quick Instructions


Mix:Stir the yogurt, garlic, ginger, chili powder, garam masala, and salt in a small bowl.
Toss the chicken cubes in this mix. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
2. Cook Chicken:Melt half of the butter in a small pan over medium heat.
Fry the chicken until it turns brown on the outside. Scoop the chicken out onto a plate.
3. Make Sauce:Put the rest of the butter into the same pan. Fry the onion until soft.
Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cumin for 30 seconds. Pour in the tomato puree and simmer for 5 minutes
.4. Finish:Stir in the cream and sugar. Put the chicken back into the sauce. Turn the heat to low.
Let it simmer for 5 more minutes until the chicken is cooked through.

Recipe-3 - Egg Fried Rice

Quick Instructions


1. Fry Garlic:
Heat the oil or butter in a very small pan over medium heat.
Fry the garlic for 20 seconds.2. Heat Rice:Add the cold rice.
Break up any clumps. Stir-fry for 1 minute until warm.
3. Cook Egg:
Push the rice to one side of the pan. Pour the beaten egg into the empty space.
Scramble the egg quickly until cooked.
4. Mix and Season:
Mix the egg into the rice. Drizzle the soy sauce and add a tiny pinch of salt.
Stir everything together for 30 seconds and serve.
